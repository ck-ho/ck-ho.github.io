TAPAS TOKKI.

In my work, I believe in conveying a strong and structured aesthetic that can transcend mediums. My collaboration with Tapas Tokki involved transforming the atmospheric, charged vibe of the dining space into digital illustration. Chef Jin Jeong crafts a holistic experience for his space that balances laid-back vibes with a quiet energy, and I wanted to impart the same impression through my interpretation of his restaurant.
