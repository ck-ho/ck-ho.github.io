FRESH - grow your habits.

INTRODUCTION.

FRESH was a collarboratve design project from my spring 2020 prototyping class. Our team of five conducted creative ideation, user interviews, and user testing to produce a prototype of a fitness and nutrition app. At the time of the project, stay at home orders as a result of the COVID-19 pandemic were just recently implemented. These orders created significant disruption in the athletic and nutritional habits of many people. As such, the goal of our app was to assist those under stay at home orders in their health and nutrition efforts.

MY RESPONSIBLITIES

  • Defining core features to avoid creep
  • Ideation and conception of plant concept
