NEXTALLDAY.

NEXTALLDAY was a YouTube gaming channel that was intended to focus on strong aesthetic as well as grassroots gaming communities, such as the FGC. The design system was based on a stark, monochromatic minimalism that served as a blank canvas that enabled strong design elements from individual games serve as the primary focus. In addition, the intention was to create a quiet, authoritative tone to differentiate the channel from a market that demanded loud attention and novelty.
